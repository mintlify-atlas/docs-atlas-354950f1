title: "APreds UI quickstart: enable predictions in Gorilla Tag"
description: "Install APreds UI, open the in-game overlay with the U key, enable predictions, and adjust your first setting — all in under two minutes."
---

Once APreds UI is installed, getting predictions running takes only a few key presses. Follow these steps to open the overlay, activate predictions, and dial in your first strength value.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Complete installation">
    If you have not already installed the mod, follow the [installation guide](/installation) first. Make sure `APredsUI.dll` is in `BepInEx\plugins\MO\` before launching.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Launch Gorilla Tag">
    Start Gorilla Tag through Steam. BepInEx loads APreds UI in the background — you do not need to take any action during startup.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Open the APreds UI panel">
    Once you are in-game, press **U** on your keyboard. The APreds UI overlay window appears on screen. You can press **U** again at any time to toggle it closed.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Enable predictions">
    Click the **Enable Predictions** toggle inside the panel. The toggle activates the prediction system — movement prediction is inactive until you turn this on.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Set your initial Preds Strength">
    Move the **Preds Strength** slider to choose how strongly predictions affect your movement. The valid range is `0.001` to `0.2`. Start low and increase gradually as you get comfortable.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Test it in-game">
    Move your arms in-game. You should feel the prediction effect applied to your movement. Adjust **Preds Strength** up or down until it feels right.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Tip>
  Start with a **Preds Strength** value around `0.05` before increasing. High values can feel disorienting until you understand how the prediction interacts with your movement style.
</Tip>

<Info>
  The overlay window is draggable — click and drag the title bar to reposition it anywhere on screen so it does not block your view.
</Info>
